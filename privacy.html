Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 12, 2025

SwarmSlayer is operated by Swarm Shield LLC. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the SwarmSlayer mobile application and related services.

Information We Do NOT Collect

SwarmSlayer does not collect or transmit:

  • Phone call audio or recordings
  • Live call data or call logs
  • Contacts or address book data
  • Background phone activity
  • Location data
  • Personal identifiers such as name, address, or SSN

Information You Provide

Any phone numbers or notes you enter into SwarmSlayer are:

  • Stored locally on your device only
  • Used solely to provide on-device scam awareness features
  • Never transmitted to Swarm Shield LLC servers

Reporting Features

When you choose to report a suspected scam (for example, via 7726), SwarmSlayer opens your device’s SMS application with pre-filled text. You must manually send the message.

Swarm Shield LLC does not receive or store copies of these messages.

Third-Party Services

SwarmSlayer relies on standard operating system features (such as text-to-speech and SMS apps). We do not integrate advertising SDKs or third-party analytics trackers.

Data Security

Because data remains on your device, SwarmSlayer minimizes exposure to external risks. You are responsible for securing your own device.

Children’s Privacy

SwarmSlayer is not directed toward children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated to reflect app changes or legal requirements. Updates will be posted on this page.
